The game is a hexagonal shape that includes lines of horizontal circular pieces and lines of circular slashes to the right and left. These lines are different in length according to the sides of the hexagonal shape. Each time the game is played, the player is allowed to shade only one piece and the basic idea is precedence of the shading of the last piece in the horizontal line or the italic line to the right or left. For example, when the player shade the last piece of a horizontal line consisting of 5 pieces added to his score 5 points or when the last piece of a italic line consists of 4 pieces added to his score 4 points and so until all the pieces are shaded and the winner is the highest player score
